# Threat Intelligence

## Purpose

The purpose of this process is to identify and manage emerging threats.

## Scope

- All employees and third-party users.
- All In Scope ISO27001 Systems and Technology
- The Information Security Management System (ISMS)

## ISO27001 Annex A 5.7 Threat Intelligence

- Information relating to information security threats should be collected and analysed to produce threat intelligence.

- Guidance: ISO 27001 Annex A Control 5.7 Threat intelligence

## Principles

Existing and emerging threats are identified and mitigated via the Risk Management Process and Continual Improvement Process.

## Objectives

- SAMPLE Identify emerging threats affecting our stack; brief leadership monthly; feed actionable items into risk treatment.

## Information Sources

- SAMPLE Vendor advisories, national CSIRT feeds, industry ISACs, and internal incident trends.

## Internal

- SAMPLE SIEM alerts, helpdesk tickets, post-incident reviews, and vulnerability scan results.

## External

- SAMPLE CISA advisories, GitHub security advisories, and supplier security bulletins.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- SAMPLE Security lead owns the process; engineering managers implement; executive sponsor approves reports.

## Threat Intelligence Reporting

- A Threat Intelligence Report is created.
- The Threat Intelligence Report is shared with The Management Review Team.
- The Threat Intelligence Report is shared at least at the Management Review Team Meeting and if a significant threat is identified.
- Threat Intelligence Reports are kept for at least 12 months.
- Progress of Threat Mitigation is reported via the Risk Management Process and Continual Improvement - Process as relevant.

## Threat Intelligence Report

- SAMPLE Monthly summary distributed to management; critical items escalated within 24 hours.

## Process Compliance

- SAMPLE Process followed for all starter/leaver/mover requests in the last quarter; exceptions reviewed in management review.

## Compliance Measurement

The information security management team will verify compliance to this process through various methods, including but not limited to, business tool reports, internal and external audits, and feedback to the process owner.

## Exceptions

Any exception to the process must be approved and recorded by the Information Security Manager in advance and reported to the Management Review Team.

## Non-Compliance

An employee found to have violated this process may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

## Continual Improvement

The process is updated and reviewed as part of the continual improvement process.
