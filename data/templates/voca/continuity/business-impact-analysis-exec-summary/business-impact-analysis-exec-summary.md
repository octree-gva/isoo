# Introduction

The business continuity plans, and disaster recovery plans are based on a business impact assessment. The business impact assessment looks at the impact on and of people, systems and premises.

## Business Impact Assessment Approach

- The business impact assessment is conducted at least annually or after any significant and / or material change.

- The assessment is conduct by performing interviews with subject matter and domain experts as well as key business stakeholders.

- The assessment is made against defined and set criteria for assessment.

- The assessment provides for the Recovery Time Objective and the Maximum Tolerable Period of Disruption.

- The assessment considers the companies legal and regulatory responsibilities.

- The assessment was completed by a combination of a workshop with the Management Review team and individual interviews.
